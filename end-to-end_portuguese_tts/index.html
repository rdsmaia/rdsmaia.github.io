Audio samples from "An end-to-end adaptive TTS system for Portuguese"

Abstract: We proposed an end-to-end multispeaker portuguese TTS with adaptive capabilities. The system is composed of three components: (1) a speaker embedding network; (2) a system that inverts text into spectrograms; (3) a neural vocoder based on MelGAN.
